About Me

My name is Patrick McCauley and I’ve been taking bikes apart and putting them back together again for the last 10 years. After moving to Madison in 2012 without a vehicle I was easily convinced by my best friend to bike everywhere instead of driving or relying on public transportation. He helped me buy a xer-upper through internet personal ads and taught me how to completely overhaul it and I’ve been xing all of my own, my friends’, and family’s bikes ever since.

I’m a slow rolling commuter cyclist and o - road tourer. I’ve done most of the Great Divide Mountain Bike Route, toured the west coast from Seattle to San Fransisco, crossed Costa Rica, and, of course, have done many local tours and trips.

Looking for a career change and a way to live in my dream job everyday I attended United Bicycle Institute (www.bikeschool.com) in the fall of 2022. I took all of the courses and earned all of the certi cates o ered. Now I’m certi ed as a professional shop mechanic, UBI/DT Swiss certi ed advanced wheel builder, and a certi ed suspension technician. I take pride in and had a blast earning these certi cations and will certainly be keeping up with continuing education!
